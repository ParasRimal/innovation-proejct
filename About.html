Our Commitment to
Sustainable Food
Distribution

At FoodLinker, our mission is to revolutionize food distribution by creating a user-friendly platform that connects food suppliers with receivers. We are dedicated to ensuring that surplus food is efficiently utilized, significantly reducing food waste. Our core values include sustainability, where we promote eco-friendly practices, and innovation, as we leverage advanced technologies like the ChatGPT API to predict expiry dates accurately. By fostering a strong community of suppliers and receivers, we aim to make a positive impact on food distribution and contribute to a more sustainable future.
